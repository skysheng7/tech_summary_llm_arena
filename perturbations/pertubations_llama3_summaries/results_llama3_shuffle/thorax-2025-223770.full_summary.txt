The study aims to develop and validate a new algorithm for predicting severe exacerbations of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). The algorithm was developed using data from the Clinical Practice Research Datalink (CPRD) and validated on a separate dataset. The algorithm also showed good calibration and discrimination performance. The study found that the algorithm performed well in predicting severe exacerbations, with an area under the receiver operating characteristic curve (AUC) of 0.87.
