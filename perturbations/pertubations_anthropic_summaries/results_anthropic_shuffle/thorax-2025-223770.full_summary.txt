This study developed and validated **PRECISE-X**, a risk prediction model that estimates the likelihood of a first severe COPD exacerbation requiring hospitalization in newly diagnosed patients. The model requires only four mandatory predictors (age, sex, FEV‚ÇÅ, and MRC dyspnoea score) plus 28 optional clinical variables, making it practical for routine clinical use. Using data from 219,015 UK primary care patients (2004-2022), the model demonstrated excellent predictive accuracy with a c-statistic of 0.836 for 5-year prediction and 0.756 for 1-year prediction.
